# PRODUCT.md — Aftermeet

**Register:** mixed. Landing/marketing = **brand**. App UI + extension = **product**.

## Product purpose
Turn meetings into execution. Transcripts in → action items, decisions,
deadlines, risks, follow-up emails, and professional Meeting Minutes out.
The core value: convert ephemeral, messy talk into a permanent, precise record
of decisions and the work that follows.

## Users
Operators who live in meetings: founders, agency/consulting leads, sales, ops.
They are competent, time-poor, and allergic to busywork. They judge tools in
seconds and trust calm precision over flash. They reread minutes the next
morning to clear tasks.

## Brand & tone
Calm, exact, quietly confident. The voice of a great chief-of-staff: never
loud, never cute, always one step ahead. Premium the way good stationery or a
well-set briefing is premium, not the way a neon SaaS hero is.

## Core metaphor
The executive briefing / dossier. The product is the *record of decisions*.
Minutes are a formal document and should read like one.

## Anti-references (do NOT look like these)
- Generic neutral-gray + blue-accent + Inter-everywhere AI SaaS (first reflex).
- Trendy "editorial" sites that slap a serif on everything (second reflex).
- ChatGPT / chatbot UI. There is no chat box anywhere.
- Crypto/neon, gradient-soaked heroes, glassmorphism, dashboard templates.

## Strategic principles
1. Intelligence is embedded in the workflow, never a chat surface.
2. Honesty about uncertainty (confidence scores, source quotes) is a feature.
3. Restraint is the aesthetic. Premium = precision + warmth + one confident accent.
4. Every surface should pass the test: nobody could say "an AI made this."
